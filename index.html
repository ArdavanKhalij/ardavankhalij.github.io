About

I'm Ardavan Khalij. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) from Vrije Universiteit Brussel and a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the University of Kashan. My research sits at the intersection of AI, Computational Creativity, and Music Informatics.

My master's thesis explored modelling, analysis, and generation of Iranian traditional music using computational creativity and machine learning (supervisors: Prof. Dr. Geraint Wiggins and Dr. Nicholas Harley). I am currently developing multiple papers based on this work; one has been presented at the International Conference on Computational & Cognitive Musicology.
